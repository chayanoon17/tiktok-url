Privacy Policy

Application: auto • Last updated: May 16, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the auto application ("the App", "we", "our") handles information in connection with its integration with the TikTok platform. The App is a personal automation tool built on n8n that allows the account owner to upload and publish video content to their own TikTok account without manual intervention.

The App is operated solely for the personal use of its owner. It does not serve third-party end users, does not collect personal data from any external party, and does not share any information with third parties.

2. TikTok API Scopes Requested

The App requests the following TikTok API permissions solely to enable automated video publishing:

Scope Purpose Data Accessed
video.upload Upload video files to TikTok via the Content Posting API Video file binary data provided by the owner's n8n workflow
video.publish Publish uploaded videos to the owner's TikTok profile Post metadata (caption, privacy settings) set by the owner

No other scopes are requested. Scopes are used exclusively for the operations described above on the account owner's own TikTok profile.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) of any user.
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services.
  • We do not store TikTok access tokens or refresh tokens beyond the operational memory of the n8n workflow instance controlled by the owner.
  • We do not retain any video content after it has been successfully submitted to TikTok's servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer any data to outside parties. Video data and metadata are transmitted only to TikTok's official API endpoints (api.tiktok.com) in accordance with TikTok's own data handling practices. No data is shared with any other third party, service, or organization.

5. Data Retention

The App does not maintain a database or persistent storage. OAuth credentials are held transiently within the owner's self-hosted n8n instance and are protected by the security measures of that instance. The owner is responsible for securing their own infrastructure.

6. Security

All API communications use HTTPS / TLS encryption as required by TikTok's platform policies. OAuth 2.0 authorization flows follow TikTok's documented security requirements, including PKCE where applicable.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction). No information is knowingly collected from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this section will reflect any revisions. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
